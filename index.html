The Construction Payment Trap

The way construction payments work today hurts both sides. We fixed it.

❌ Old Way

  • • Homeowner pays 50% upfront — contractor might disappear
  • • Contractor finishes work — homeowner delays final payment for months
  • • Disputes over "quality" lead to lawsuits and liens
  • • No verifiable history — every new project is a leap of faith

✅ GCSC Way

  • • Funds locked in escrow — contractor cannot access until verified
  • • Payment releases automatically after milestone approval
  • • AI + inspection verification removes subjective disputes
  • • On-chain reputation — every project builds permanent track record

Three Steps. Zero Trust Issues.

1

Post Your Project

Describe your construction needs, set budget, timeline, and milestones. Get matched with verified contractors in your area.

2

Fund Escrow

Homeowner deposits funds into XPR blockchain escrow. Money is visible but untouchable until verification. Contractor starts work with confidence.

3

Verify & Release

At each milestone, AI + optional inspector verifies completion. Homeowner approves. Payment releases automatically to contractor.
